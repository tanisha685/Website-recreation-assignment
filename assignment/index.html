Your Questions, Our Answers

Everything you need to know about CoFounds – how it works, why it's different, and how it helps you land better.

What is CoFounds?

CoFounds is a modern job search companion that helps you track, discover, and apply to jobs smarter – all in one place, powered by community validation and proof of work.

How is CoFounds different from LinkedIn?

Unlike LinkedIn which focuses on networking and profiles, CoFounds emphasizes proof of work and actual skills demonstration through projects and community validation.

Is Cofounds free to use?

CoFounds is completely free for job seekers. Companies pay a success fee only when they hire through our platform.
